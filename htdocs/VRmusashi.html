VR Battleship Tour

VR Battleship Tour will be my flagship project, and represents my dream for living, breathing, history in the virtual world. It also represents my time in Unity, and my ability to work in a VR environment. The goal of this project will be to simulate what it would be like to control a fully to scale 3D model of the Imperial Japanese Battleship Musashi. Musashi was part of the "Yamato" class of battleship, of which is the largest and most heavily armed and armored class of battleship to ever exist. Both Yamato and Musashi having been destroyed by American air attacks near the end of the war, existing only in living memory and limited technical documents until now.
