About

Behind the projects

My name is Peter Wright, and I am currently studying Games, Interactive Media, Mobile (GIMM) at Boise State University. For the past three years I have dedicated myself and my life to studying the technology of the future, and this website is for displaying my different work over those years. For as long as I can remember I've been fascinated with technology in all forms, and thanks to GIMM I can finally give that interest purpose. My main outlet for this technology is both history and aviation, and I hope to work in either field in the future. With the rise of VR developement, there is now an opportunity for the average person to tour and experience all sorts of history. What once lived only in memory, can now be experienced as close to physically being there as possible. I also hope to take my knowledge of augmented reality to the aerospace industry. As aircraft become more and more advanced, so too does the need to find increasingly intuitive and effective ways of displaying the litany of information pilots take in. Augmented reality has already proven its usefulness in this regard, and it is my goal to aid in its evolution in the coming years.
