Sunrise: Everyone has their own beginning.

My first ever project in GIMM, the Sunrise Project, was a simple Adobe Animate creation. Easy on the eyes? No, but it marks my first step in GIMM.

Phoenix Squadron: One step at a time.

What do you do when you learn the ropes? Push your boundaries. With developement starting just after the Sunrise Project, Phoenix Squadron is a very simple game made in Adobe Animate, yet it represents both a milestone for my time in GIMM, and a testament to how much can be learned in a short time.

VR WWII Battleship Tour: The flagship project.

This project allows anyone to virtually explore the IJN Musashi, belonging to the most powerful class of battleship ever created. This project represents my time in Unity, and developing in virtual reality.
