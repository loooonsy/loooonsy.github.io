Operation H.A.W.K

Operation H.A.W.K is a group project utilizing Xcode in order to assist children on the autistic spectrum with orginization of their daily school routine.
Unfortunately, the rapid spread of COVID-19 put many roadblocks in our ability to flesh out Operation HAWK to its fullest potential, and lacking an Apple device I was unable to play a major role in the programming of this application.
Because of this, my role in Operation H.A.W.K involved early conception of creating a heads up display, inspired by aerspace HUD technology, to aid in navigation by creating waypoints in the augmented world for the child to follow. Unable to work on this HUD element as originally planned, I offered advice and suggestions regarding project developement to my groupmates, as well as being in charge of our final showcase video.
