Size of an Atom

Size of an Atom was a group project made at the end of my first year in GIMM. The biggest difference between this and my other projects is that Atoms is designed to work with mobile devices. The goal is to visually and audibly describe the relative size of an atom, if the nucleus was the size of every day objects and places.
