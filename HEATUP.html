HEAT UP

HEAT UP is a project aimed at immersing the user in the world of armored combat, with a near-futuristic theme. The claustrophobic confines of a tank's interior are matched only by the level of protection offered; HEAT Up aims to allow anyone the opportunity to immerse themself in such an environment, with all of the benifits and drawbacks of demonstrated throughout history and the modern day.
While inside the turret, the user is provided a thermal gunsight, as well as a general vision sight, toggleable between normal and thermal views and rotatable on its own axis. The goal is to aquire targets using the higher field of view and free-look capabilities of the general vision sight, and engage using the more precise gunsight. While staying in the turret provides the best protection, cameras won't always be enough to survey the situation. Users will have to leave the safety of their steel box in order to effectively navigate, position, and build situational awareness. Gameplay centers on the balance of unrestricted vision for the sometimes costly price of protection, and presents the unique challenge for the user, who must base their decisions on the confidence they have in both their systems, as well as their ability to quickly and regularly assess the world around them.
